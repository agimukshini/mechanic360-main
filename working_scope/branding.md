

## 🚗 Branding Proposal

### **App Name:** **Mechanic360** (English) · **Mekaniku360** (Albanian)

> “The 360° view of every vehicle, every visit, every part.”

---

## 🔹 Brand Identity Overview

| Element                  | Recommendation                                                         | Reason                                                                               |
| ------------------------ | ---------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Primary Color**        | ⚙️ **#0077B6 (Deep Mechanic Blue)**                                    | Feels technical, trustworthy, and complements ManageFleet’s theme (blue/gray tones). |
| **Secondary Color**      | 🔧 **#00B4D8 (Cyan Accent)**                                           | Adds modern energy and connects to digital/tech ecosystem.                           |
| **Neutral / Background** | **#F5F5F5 (light gray)** + **#1B263B (charcoal)**                      | Industrial yet clean, fits garage and cloud themes.                                  |
| **Typography**           | **"Inter" or "Poppins"**                                               | Both are readable, clean, and modern — suitable for web & mobile.                    |
| **Logo Symbol**          | A **gear integrated with a QR code pattern** forming a **360° circle** | Visually ties together inspection, precision, and digital traceability.              |
| **Logo Text**            | “Mechanic360” / “Mekaniku360” or “M360”                               | Compact and scalable for dashboards, mobile, and stickers.                           |
| **App Icon**             | Simplified gear-circle + QR pixel corner                               | Looks sharp in small icons and printed labels.                                       |
| **Tagline**              | “Smart Maintenance. Real Mechanics.”                                   | Positions it as both digital and workshop-friendly.                                  |

---

## 🔹 Tone & Style

* **Professional, mechanical, and data-driven**
* UI should feel like a **digital workshop board** (dark mode optional)
* Minimal clutter — **icons, badges, and QR elements** as visual language
* **Integrates visually with ManageFleet** but with a more **hands-on** vibe

---

## 🔹 Integration Branding Strategy

| System          | Visual Identity                                    | Function                                                     |
| --------------- | -------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **ManageFleet** | Fleet Management → Corporate / Administrative look | Manages vehicles & assets globally                           |
| **Mechanic360** | Maintenance Execution → Workshop / Technical look  | Performs physical maintenance, inspections, and service logs |

To link them visually:

* Use the same **font family and button styles**
* Keep **blue/cyan palette continuity**
* Add **QR-code motif** to tie the “physical-digital bridge” between the two
